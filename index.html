Do You Sudoku?

The game Sudoku was created by a Japanese businessman named Maki Kaji. Kaji was born in Sapporo, Japan on October 8th, 1951. He was wdiely known as the "father of Sudoku", after creating the game in the 1980s.

The name Sudoku was shortened from the original name "Suuji wa dokushin ni kagiru", or "numbers should be single". This particular Sudoku game was made in homour of the life of Maki Kaji, after his unfortunate death on August 10th 2021.
